How ForgeUp Works

Core Philosophy: Consistency Over Cramming

ForgeUp is built on the scientific principle that distributed practice (studying consistently every day) leads to significantly better long-term memory retention and academic success compared to massed practice (cramming all night before an exam). Our platform gamifies your daily study habits, rewarding disciplined, regular sessions through interactive stats, level progress, badges, and streaks.

Timer Modes & Study Zones

We provide two distinct modes inside the Study Session Zone to accommodate different productivity styles:

  • Normal Timer: A stopwatch mode that counts upward from 00:00:00. This is ideal for open-ended study blocks where you want to work continuously and pause or stop manually when you are finished.
  • Pomodoro Timer: A structured mode based on the Pomodoro Technique. It counts down and automatically alternates between focus periods (e.g., 25, 50, or 90 minutes) and break periods (5, 10, or 20 minutes). Transition notifications alert you with visual changes to guide your session.

XP, Leveling, and Streak Mechanics

Your efforts are tracked and rewarded using several gamified variables:

⚡ XP & Leveling

You earn 1 XP for every 1 minute spent in active focus. Completing custom study targets awards a bonus of 50 XP. Accumulating XP advances your student level dynamically.

🔥 Streak Preservation

Your daily streak increases by 1 for each calendar day you complete at least one session of 10+ seconds. Failing to record a session resets your streak to 0, which directly impacts your rank multipliers.

📈 Consistency Score

This dynamic score (0–100) measures your long-term study patterns, factoring in consecutive active days, weekly target completion rates, and historical study session regularity.

🏆 Global Leaderboard

Ranks are computed using a balanced composite formula: 40% Consistency Score + 30% Study Time + 20% Active Streak Length + 10% Unlocked Badges. Consistency is heavily weighted to prevent cramming.

Leaderboard Standings: All-Time vs. Seasonal

You can toggle between two separate views on the leaderboard to compare your performance with other students:

  • All-Time Standings: This view ranks users based on their entire study history since registration. The "Study Time" component of the ranking score is calculated using your total lifetime study minutes, and your overall lifetime XP is displayed.
  • Seasonal Standings: This view resets at the start of every calendar month. It isolates rankings to show who is currently active and consistent this month. The "Study Time" component is calculated using only focus sessions recorded during the current calendar month. It displays your seasonal XP, giving new users a fair chance to climb to the top of the ranks every single month.
  • Privacy Masking: If you disable "Public Leaderboard Visibility" in your settings, other users will only see your username and overall rank. Your levels, XP, streaks, and focus metrics will be masked with N/A or hidden on the standings table to protect your privacy.

Anti-Cheat & Session Validation

To preserve leaderboard integrity, ForgeUp runs real-time server-side checks. Overlapping study logs, artificial session durations, duplicate submissions, and client-side clock tampering are automatically flagged and rejected. Active sessions survive browser crashes and page refreshes by synchronizing client states with the server database.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

At ForgeUp, we are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we protect it, and how you can exercise control over your data.

1. Information We Collect

We only collect the minimal amount of data necessary to provide a functional and secure experience:

  • Account Information: Your username, email address, and hashed password. Email addresses are encrypted using industry-standard AES-256-GCM encryption before being written to our database.
  • Study Session Logs: Duration of focus blocks, subject categories, session timestamps, and optional user notes.
  • Academic Goals: Targets created, target descriptions, scope parameters, and completion records.
  • Audit & Security Logs: Active session heartbeats, failed login attempts, and rate-limiting metrics to monitor platform security.

2. How We Protect Your Data

We implement a robust security architecture to protect your details from unauthorized access:

  • All communication is protected by HTTP security headers and Cross-Site Request Forgery (CSRF) tokens.
  • Emails are encrypted at-rest using cryptographic keys kept separate from public endpoints.
  • We store passwords using secure hashing algorithms, making it impossible for administrators or external parties to read your actual password.

3. Profile Visibility & Leaderboard Masking

By default, registered users participate in the Global Standings. Your level, total study hours, current streak, and badges are visible to other logged-in students. You can disable this by toggling Public Leaderboard Visibility in your Settings. In private mode, other users will only see your username and overall rank, and all your detailed statistics will be masked.

4. Cookies & Session Management

ForgeUp uses a single, secure session cookie (forgeup.sid) configured with HttpOnly and SameSite=Strict flags. This cookie is used strictly to identify your authenticated state and recover active study timers in the event of browser crashes. We do not use tracking or advertising cookies.

5. Data Ownership, Portability, and Deletion

You have full ownership of your educational history. You can exercise your rights at any time directly through the Profile panel:

  • Data Export: Clicking "Download My Data" generates a complete, structured JSON export of all your account history, including milestones, goals, and study sessions.
  • Account Deletion: Clicking "Delete Account" permanently deletes your profile, encrypted email, hashed credentials, study history, and backups from our databases. This action is irreversible.
